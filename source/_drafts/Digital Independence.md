# Digital Independence

1. I own the content, and it is easily portable
2. Low-cost
3. Easy to update and maintain


This has been made front of mind by the current saga surrounding  WordPress (Matt Mullenweg specifically) and WP Engine

What happens when the owners actions negatively impact you

The web is a never-ending frontier, and it is increasingly easy to stake your claim to a small portion of it, often for free (or, at least, a very small upfront investment).

I'm a tech-savvy individual, but want to use my experience to try and demystify the process. I'm not building a new blogging platform - rather distilling my experience down to some simple instructions that anyone can follow. (Similar to Derek Sivers Tech Independence).
